Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

My salon is based at my home in Newton Farm, Cambuslang. The street address is 24 Ewe Avenue, G72 6WB.

How can I pay?

I accept cash, bank transfer, card and contactless.

Do I need to leave a deposit when booking?

Currently I don't take deposits and I hope this can continue.

Is there parking?

Yes, parking is available at the front of the house or in the driveway.

What time should I arrive for my appointment?

Try to arrive around 5-10 minutes before your appointment to allow full use of the treatment time.

I'm running late, what should I do?

If you're running late please get in touch where possible and I will do my best to still fit you in for your full treatment, however this is not always possible and we may have to reduce your treatment time or reschedule your appointment.
